ABOUT

Hi, I'm Dianne Eramo. I love to cook and I love flavors from around the world, so this blog is a collection of delicious dishes that may have their origins in countries around the world, or just in my kitchen. I'll often think of some dish I like to eat, search for recipes, combine a couple, and add extra spices for more flavor. When I'm not cooking, I enjoy doing a social dance called lindy hop, traveling, learning to play my ukelele, and learning web development.

If you'd like to follow me on Twitter, my username is @dianneeramo
